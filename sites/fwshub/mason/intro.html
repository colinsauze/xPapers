This Climate Change Consortium Wales online research guide (C3W-publications), is based on a collection of online academic papers and books as well as articles in the popular media. Together with annotations from acknowledged experts and users' discussion forums, this forms a guide aimed at researchers, students, policy-makers and the business community.
