While we prefer the RSS method, you can use any of the following:
  • RSS feeds with PRISM tags (best method)
  • Bibliographic files (e.g. BibTeX)
The editors will consider each request to monitor a journal individually. The decision will be based on the relevance and the standing of the journal.
